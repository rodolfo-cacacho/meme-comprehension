What is MemeQA?

MemeQA is a crowd-sourced meme understanding dataset designed to evaluate Vision-Language Models (VLLMs) in their comprehension of internet memes and human humor. This benchmark tests AI systems' ability to understand cultural references, visual-textual context, and the nuanced layers of meaning that make memes effective communication tools in digital culture.

The dataset is being developed by researchers at THWS (Technische Hochschule WÃ¼rzburg-Schweinfurt) and CAIRO (Center for AI and Robotics) to address the growing need for AI systems that can understand not just what they see, but the cultural and humorous context behind visual content.

Research Goals:
  • Evaluate VLLM meme comprehension
  • Benchmark humor understanding in AI
  • Create diverse cultural context dataset
  • Enable explainable meme analysis
Applications:
  • Social media content moderation
  • Cultural trend analysis
  • Humor-aware AI assistants
  • Digital literacy tools
